**Sage Quick Meet** is a streamlined video calling platform designed for instant, hassle-free 1-on-1 video meetings. Inspired by Google Meet's simplicity, it eliminates the need for accounts or complex setup - just create or join a room and start talking.

Built with **React** and **WebRTC** for real-time peer-to-peer communication, styled with **Tailwind CSS**, and powered by **Socket.IO** for reliable signaling.

## Key Features

- **Instant Video Calls**
  - No account registration required
  - Quick room creation and joining
  - Direct peer-to-peer connection
  - High-quality video and audio

- **Anonymous & Private**
  - No personal data collection
  - Temporary meeting rooms
  - Secure WebRTC connections
  - End-to-end encrypted streams

- **User-Friendly Interface**
  - Clean, intuitive design
  - Simple room code sharing
  - One-click meeting creation
  - Responsive across all devices
